Who am i

There are hundreds of thousands of Professional Developers you’ll come across.

You might be wondering, “What’s so special about this guy? Why should I consider him for my project(s)?” You need someone who can help you get into the mind of your target audience. In addition, hiring someone who can help you focus on your audience’s pain points will help you ensure that you’re delivering the absolute best. And that’s something I have been doing since 2 years. Firstly, after carefully analyzing your requirements, I will be scheduling a call/chat with you and coming up with a few questions to have a better understanding of your project. I’ll proceed towards carrying out thorough research about your target audience and will be sharing my findings. Once that’s done, and I am clear on the requirements, I will proceed towards the Design phase, followed by Development, Writing & Promotion. To know more about my skills, feel free to check out my skillset.
